Project Overview

This professional dashboard showcases a hybrid renewable energy system that integrates solar and wind power with battery storage. Live (simulated) data demonstrates real-time monitoring of power generation, storage behaviour, and environmental benefits. This project is designed and developed as a final year work at Sri Sairam College of Engineering.
